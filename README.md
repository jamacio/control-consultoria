# Control Consultoria

**Making business management simple for entrepreneurs.**

Control Consultoria was born with the purpose of helping small and medium-sized business owners overcome management challenges and understand the corporate world in a simple, practical, and straightforward way.

Founded by Viviane Andrade in 2019, we operate as a strategic partner for companies across various segments, offering solutions in:

- Business Consulting
- Recruitment & Selection
- Strategic Management
- Tax Department
- HR & Payroll Department
- Training & Development

More than providing services, we believe in building trust-based relationships, offering close support, personalized solutions, and ongoing assistance so every business owner can focus on what truly matters: growing their business.
